About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Department of Aerospace Engineering, IISc Bangalore. My research focuses on wave propagation, nonlocal elasticity, strain-gradient mechanics, mathematical modeling, and computational mechanics.

Research Interests

  • Wave Propagation in Layered Media
  • Nonlocal Elasticity
  • Strain Gradient Mechanics
  • Computational Mechanics
  • Mathematical Modeling
  • Physics-Informed Machine Learning

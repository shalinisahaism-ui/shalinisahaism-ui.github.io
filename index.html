About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. My research focuses on wave propagation in advanced materials, nonlocal elasticity, strain-gradient mechanics, computational mechanics, and mathematical modeling. I am particularly interested in analytical and computational approaches for understanding dynamic behavior in layered and heterogeneous structures.

Research Areas

Wave Propagation

Rayleigh waves, SH waves, guided waves, layered structures and surface acoustic waves.

Nonlocal Elasticity

Eringen nonlocal theory, strain-gradient models and size-dependent continuum mechanics.

Computational Mechanics

Dynamic stiffness methods, spectral finite elements and numerical simulation.

Physics-Informed AI

Data-driven mechanics and machine learning assisted physical modeling.
